CENRO Sanitary Management System

A comprehensive sanitary management system for CENRO, Bayawan City. Streamlining environmental services for a cleaner future.

Services

  • Septage desludging
  • Grass cutting

This CENRO Sanitary Management System collects information you provide when you register and when you submit service requests (for example name, address, contact details, and map location for septage desludging or grass cutting).

Data is used to process your request, schedule inspections or field work, and meet local environmental requirements. It is stored on office-controlled systems and handled in line with applicable government privacy rules.

You may review or update your profile when signed in. For questions about your personal data, contact CENRO Bayawan City using the details in the Contact section.

By using this portal you agree to provide accurate information and to follow procedures set by the City Environment and Natural Resources Office (CENRO), Bayawan City.

Services depend on eligibility, inspection where required, payment of applicable fees, and staff availability. The office may update workflows or fees in line with local policy; continued reasonable use of the system after changes constitutes acceptance of those updates.

The system is provided as a convenience; if you need help with a request, contact CENRO staff directly.

This application supports transparent delivery of environmental services in Bayawan City: request status, fees, and processing steps are recorded in the system for authorized staff and for consumers in connection with their own requests.

The Transparency Seal reflects the office’s commitment to accountable service. For questions about procedures, records, or complaints, please contact CENRO Bayawan City.
